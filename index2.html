About Me

I am a doctoral candidate at the Technical University of Munich (TUM), Germany, conducting my research in the Medical Autonomy and Precision Surgery (MAPS) Lab. My work is supervised by Prof. Dr.-Ing Ali Nasseri and Prof. Nassir Navab. My primary research interests lie in robotic medical autonomy, the application of AI in therapies, and the design of embedded systems.

Prior to my doctoral studies, I obtained both my Bachelor's and Master's degrees from Sun Yat-Sen University, China, under the supervision of Prof. Huang Kai.

Research Projects

Design of Ophthalmic Robot

Our team is developing a high-precision ophthalmic surgical robot. Notably, we are one of the few teams to possess eye-surgery robots with independent intellectual property. We are actively seeking students for thesis projects (Bachelor/Master), clinical projects (KAP), or other research collaborations. Please feel free to contact me if you are interested.

Medical AI Application

We are focused on creating intelligent algorithms to automate the actions of our ophthalmic surgical robot, enhancing its precision and capabilities during surgical procedures.

Medical Image Analysis

This research area involves leveraging Artificial Intelligence to analyze ophthalmic images. The goal is to improve diagnostic accuracy and assist in the planning of treatments for various eye conditions.
