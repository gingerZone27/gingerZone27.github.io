Design of Ophthalmic Robot ------
(Open Jobs: 2)
We are designing an ophthalmic robot as well as its frame in the surgical scenario. Currently there are two major problems to be solved: visual-based status recognition, and kinematic calibration during its RCM movement. Our target is to implement a RCM-enabled robot with its surgical precision exceeding that of human surgeons.
